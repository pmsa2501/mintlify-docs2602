### Frequently Asked Questions

#### What if I forgot my Google password?

Use Google's password recovery at accounts.google.com. MAM360 uses your Google account, so Google manages your password.

#### What if I don't receive the email code?

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait 2 minutes - sometimes email is delayed
3. Click "Resend Code" (Reenviar codigo)
4. Make sure you entered the correct email address

#### Can I use any email address?

Yes, any valid email address works. You don't need a Google account to use email sign-in.

#### Why can't I access certain features?

Your access depends on your role. Clients cannot access PRC operations or modify trucks in Estado 2 or higher. Contact your administrator if you need additional access.

#### How do I change my phone number?

Currently, phone numbers can only be changed through the setup process. Contact your administrator for assistance.

#### Is my information secure?

Yes. MAM360 uses industry-standard security:

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Secure session management
- Role-based access control
- Your data is only accessible to you and authorized administrators

### Troubleshooting

#### "Session expired" error

Your session timed out. Simply log in again.

#### "Access denied" error

You tried to use a feature not available to your role. This is normal - different roles have different permissions.

#### Can't receive email codes

1. Check spam folder
2. Wait 5 minutes and try again
3. Contact your administrator

#### Page won't load after login

1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Contact technical support

### Important Notes

- Always sign out when using shared computers
- Keep your Google account secure with a strong password
- If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact your administrator immediately
- Your role determines what features you can use - this is by design to protect port operations

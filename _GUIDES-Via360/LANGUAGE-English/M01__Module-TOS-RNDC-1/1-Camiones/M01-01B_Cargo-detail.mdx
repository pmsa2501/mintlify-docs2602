## Feature Overview

### What is the Detail Sheet?

The Detail Sheet is a panel that appears when you click on any row in the Camiones (trucks) table. It shows all the information about that transaction and allows authorized users to make changes like updating the status, registering problems, or uploading documents.

---

## Step-by-Step Usage

### How to Open the Detail Sheet

#### Step 1: Navigate to Camiones

From the dashboard, click on **Transporte** \> **Camiones** in the sidebar menu.

#### Step 2: Click on a Row

Click anywhere on the row for the transaction you want to view. The Detail Sheet will slide open from the right side of the screen.

#### Step 3: View Transaction Information

The Detail Sheet is organized into four columns:

| Column | Information |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Fechas y Horas** | Timestamps for each estado (status) change |
| **Cliente/Producto** | Customer name, product type, and pile number |
| **Empresa/Conductor/Camion** | Transport company, driver info, and truck details |
| **Documentos/PRC** | Attached documents and port control information |

#### Step 4: Close the Detail Sheet

Click the **X** button in the top right corner, or click outside the panel to close it.

### How to Change the Estado (Status)

> **Note**: Only port staff (Operators and Administrators) can change estados from Estado 2 onwards. Transport company users can only modify records at Estados 0 and 1.

#### Step 1: Click the Estado Badge

The current estado is shown as a colored badge at the top of the Detail Sheet. Click on it to open the dropdown.

#### Step 2: Select a New Estado

Choose the appropriate estado from the list. The system will save the change automatically.

#### Step 3: Verify the Change

A success message will appear, and the badge will update to show the new estado.

### How to Report a Problem (X1 Novedad abierta)

If a truck has a problem during the process (Estados 2-8), you can register it using the X1 button.

#### Step 1: Click "Registrar Novedad (X1)"

This button only appears for transactions at Estados 2 through 8.

#### Step 2: Enter a Description

In the modal that opens, describe the problem. This field is required.

#### Step 4: Click "Registrar X1"

The transaction will be marked as having an open problem (Estado 101).

### How to Close a Problem (X2 Novedad cerrada)

If a truck leaves without completing the process, use the X2 button.

#### Step 1: Click " (X2) Cerrar"

This button only appears for transactions at Estados 2 through 8.

#### Step 2: Enter a Description

Explain why the truck is being marked as abandoned.

#### Step 3: Click "Registrar X2"

The transaction will be closed as abandoned (Estado 102). This is a terminal state - no further changes are possible.

### How to Select a Bascula (Weighing Scale)

When a truck reaches the weighing station, operators must record which scale was used.

#### Bascula de Entrada (Entry Weight)

- Available at Estado 7 and above

#### Bascula de Salida (Exit Weight)

- Available at Estado 8 and above

### How to Upload Documents

Documents can be added or replaced in the **Adjuntos** section.

#### Step 1: Find the Document Slot

Seven document types are available:

1. Orden de Despacho
2. Remesa
3. Manifiesto Electrónico
4. Hoja Tiempos
5. Conductor ID
6. Pago Seguridad Social
7. Otros Documentos (Other Documents)

#### Step 2: Click "Subir documento"

Click the upload button next to the document you want to add.

#### Step 3: Select a File

Choose a file from your computer. Allowed formats:

- **PDF** (Preferred for documents)
- **JPG, PNG, WebP** (For images)
- **Maximum size**: 10 MB

#### Step 4: Wait for Upload

The file will upload and the button will change to show "Ver documento" (View Document).

### How to View RNDC Preview

For transactions at Estado 7 or above, you can preview the data that will be reported to the RNDC system.

#### Step 1: Click "Preview RNDC Payload"

This button appears in the Detail Sheet for qualifying transactions.

#### Step 2: Review the Payload

The modal shows:

- **Sistema Enturnamiento ID**: The product code for RNDC
- **RIEN Tabs**: Different report types based on estado (RIEN2 at Estado 7, RIEN1 at Estado 8)
- **Validation**: Shows if any required fields are missing (in amber)
- **Demo Mode Alert**: Indicates actual submission is disabled for testing

#### Step 3: Close the Modal

Click outside the modal or press the X button to close.

## Reference

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Why can't I change the estado?

- **If you're a Client/Transport Company**: You can only modify records at Estados 0 and 1. Once a truck enters the port queue (Estado 2\+), only port staff can make changes.
- **If you're an Operator**: Ensure you're logged in with the correct role. Contact your administrator if issues persist.

#### Why don't I see the X1/X2 buttons?

The X1 and X2 buttons only appear for transactions at Estados 2 through 8. If the transaction is at Estado 0, 1, 9, or already at X1/X2, these buttons are hidden.

#### What happens if I select X1/X2 from the dropdown?

The system will show an error message asking you to use the dedicated buttons instead. This ensures you provide the required description.

#### Why can't I select Bascula?

- **Bascula de Entrada**: Only appears at Estado 7 or above
- **Bascula de Salida**: Only appears at Estado 8 or above
- Only Operators and Administrators have permission to select basculas

#### What file types can I upload?

PDF, JPG, PNG, and WebP files up to 10 MB are accepted.

#### Can I undo an estado change?

Contact your administrator. Some transitions may be reversible depending on your permissions and business rules.

### Troubleshooting

- **If upload fails**: Check your file size (must be under 10MB) and format (PDF, JPG, PNG, WebP only)
- **If estado change fails**: Contact your administrator - you may not have permission for that transition
- **If the page doesn't load**: Refresh your browser and try again
- **For other issues**: Contact system support

### Important Notes

- Changes are saved automatically - you don't need to click a "Save" button
- All estado changes are logged with timestamps
- Documents can be replaced by uploading a new file to the same slot
- The Detail Sheet shows the most current data - close and reopen if changes were made elsewhere

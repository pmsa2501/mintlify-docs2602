## Feature Overview
The Detail Sheet opens when you click any row in the Camiones table. It keeps you in-context while you review timestamps, participants, estado controls, documents, and RNDC reporting.

The panel is organized into four columns:

| Column | Contents |
| --- | --- |
| **Fechas y Horas** | Timestamps for each estado change |
| **Cliente / Producto** | Customer name, product type, pile number |
| **Empresa / Conductor / Camión** | Transport company, driver info, truck details |
| **Documentos / PRC** | Attached documents and port control data |

## How To

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Open the Detail Sheet">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Camiones">
        From the sidebar, click **Transporte → Camiones**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Open a transaction row">
        Click anywhere on the transaction row you want to inspect. The Detail Sheet slides in from the right.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Close when done">
        Click the **X** in the top-right corner, or click outside the panel.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change the Estado (Status)">
    <Note>
      Only **Operators** and **Administrators** can change estados from Estado 2 onward. Transport company users (Clients) may only modify records at Estados 0 and 1.
    </Note>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the estado dropdown">
        Click the colored estado badge at the top of the panel.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select a new estado">
        Choose from the available estados. The change saves automatically.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Confirm the update">
        A success toast appears and the badge updates.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      **X1 and X2 cannot be selected from this dropdown.** They require their dedicated buttons to ensure a problem description is entered. Attempting to select them here triggers an error toast.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Report a Problem — (X1) Registrar Novedad">
    <Info>
      The X1 and X2 buttons are only visible for transactions at **Estados 2 through 8**.
    </Info>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click '(X1) Registrar Novedad'">
        The button appears in the detail panel for qualifying estados.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enter a description">
        Describe the problem. **This field is required.**
      </Step>

      <Step title="Submit X1">
        Click **Registrar X1**. The transaction moves to **Estado X1 (Novedad Abierta)**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Close a Problem — (X2) Abandonar">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click '(X2) Abandonar'">
        Available for transactions at Estados 2 through 8.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Enter a description">
        Explain the reason. **This field is required.**
      </Step>

      <Step title="Submit X2">
        Click **Registrar X2**. The transaction closes at **Estado X2 (Abandonado)**. This state is terminal — no further changes are possible.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Select a Báscula (Weighing Scale)">
    <Note>
      Only **Operators** and **Administrators** can select básculas.
    </Note>

    When a truck reaches the weighing station, record which scale was used:

    | Báscula | Available at |
    | --- | --- |
    | Báscula de Entrada | Estado 7 and above |
    | Báscula de Salida | Estado 8 and above |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Upload Documents">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Adjuntos">
        Scroll to the **Adjuntos** section.

        Seven document slots are available:
        - Orden de Despacho
        - Remesa
        - Manifiesto Electrónico
        - Hoja Tiempos
        - Conductor ID
        - Pago Seguridad Social
        - Otros Documentos
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose a slot to upload">
        Click **Subir documento** next to the slot you want to fill.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Select a file">
        Accepted formats: **PDF**, **JPG**, **PNG**, **WebP** — maximum **10 MB**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Confirm upload completed">
        The button changes to **Ver documento** once the upload is complete.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      To replace a document, upload a new file to the same slot. All documents are currently optional.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Preview the RNDC Payload">
    Available for transactions at Estado 7 or above.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open preview">
        Click **Preview RNDC Payload**.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review the modal">
        Check:
        - **Sistema Enturnamiento ID** — product code for RNDC
        - **RIEN tabs** — RIEN2 at Estado 7, RIEN1 at Estado 8
        - **Validation** — amber warnings for missing required fields
        - **Demo Mode alert** — confirms actual submission is disabled
      </Step>

      <Step title="Close the modal">
        Click outside or press X. Do not click **Verificar Conexion** in demo or staging environments.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I change the estado?">
    If you're a **Client**, estado changes are locked after Estado 1 — port staff only from Estado 2 onward. If you're an **Operator**, verify your role in Settings or contact your administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why don't I see the X1/X2 buttons?">
    These buttons only render for transactions at Estados 2–8. They are hidden at Estados 0, 1, 9, and when already at X1 or X2.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I try to select X1/X2 from the dropdown?">
    The system rejects the selection and shows an error toast. Use the dedicated **(X1) Registrar Novedad** or **(X2) Abandonar** buttons instead — they ensure the required description is entered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't I select a báscula?">
    Báscula de Entrada requires Estado 7 or above. Báscula de Salida requires Estado 8 or above. Only Operators and Admins have access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file types can I upload?">
    PDF, JPG, PNG, and WebP — up to 10 MB per file.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I undo an estado change?">
    Some transitions are reversible depending on your role and business rules. Contact your administrator.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my upload fails?">
    Check that the file is under 10 MB and in an accepted format (PDF, JPG, PNG, WebP). If the issue persists, contact system support.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Changes are saved automatically — no Save button needed. All estado changes are logged with timestamps. Close and reopen the Detail Sheet to see changes made elsewhere.
</Info>

# Profile Settings

Learn how to view and manage your account and company information.

---

## Access Settings

1. From any page, click **Ajustes** in the left sidebar under the **Usuario** section.
2. The Settings page opens with the **Perfil** tab active by default.

---

## Perfil Tab

The Perfil tab displays your personal account information.

You will see the following fields:

- **Correo electrónico** — your registered email, marked **verificado**
- **Teléfono** — your mobile number, marked **verificado**
- **Cédula** — your national ID number
- **Nombre** — your first name
- **Apellido** — your last name

### Save Changes

Click **Guardar Cambios** to save any updates.

> In demo mode, a green confirmation toast will appear: _"Cambios guardados (modo demo)"_

<Note>
  The edit pencil icons next to each field are currently in development. Clicking them will show a "Edición en desarrollo" notice.
</Note>

---

## Compañía Tab

The Compañía tab displays your organization's information.

1. Click the **Compañía** tab at the top of the Settings page.
2. You will see your company details:
   - **Nombre** — company name (e.g. CMPX 575)
   - **NIT** — tax identification number
   - **Dirección** — registered address
   - **Teléfono** — company phone number

### Save Changes

Click **Guardar Cambios** to save any updates.

<Note>
  The **Cargar imagen** button for uploading a company logo is currently in development.
</Note>

---

## Need Help?

Contact your platform administrator if any of your profile information needs to be corrected.

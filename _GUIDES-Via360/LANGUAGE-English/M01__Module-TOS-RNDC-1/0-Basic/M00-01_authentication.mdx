## Feature Overview

Authentication lets users access the platform either by entering Demo Mode (no account required) or by signing in to an existing account. New users may complete a short onboarding flow after the first successful sign-in.

---

## Demo mode (no account required)

Demo mode is available for users who don't have an account or who want to explore the portal for training purposes.

- Open the Demo Mode URL: `PUT_DEMO_URL_HERE`

---

## Sign In (account required)

Navigate to the login page. You will see the following screen:

**¡Bienvenido!**

__Seleccione cómo desea ingresar__

Two options are available:

- **Ingresar con Google**
- **Ingresar con correo electrónico**

### Option 1: Sign in with Google

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Steps">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start Google sign-in">
        Click **Ingresar con Google**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your Google account">
        A Google authentication window will open. Select your account and confirm.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Arrive at dashboard">
        You will be redirected to your dashboard automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    _If this is your first time, you will go through a short onboarding flow before reaching the dashboard._
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Option 2: Sign in with Email

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Step 1 — Enter your email">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose email sign-in">
        Click **Ingresar con correo electrónico**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter email">
        On the next screen, enter your email address in the **Correo electrónico** field.

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005395](/images/2026-03-24-005395-2.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Continue">
        Click **Continuar**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 2 — Verify your email">
    The platform will send a 6-digit verification code to your email.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find the code">
        Check your inbox for the code.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the code">
        On the **Revise su correo** screen, enter the code in the 6-box field.

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005396](/images/2026-03-24-005396-1.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    _Did not receive the code? Click **Reenviar código** to request a new one._
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 3 — Enter your phone number">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter phone number">
        On the **Teléfono móvil** screen, enter your Colombian mobile number.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Next">
        Click **Siguiente**.

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005391](/images/2026-03-24-005391.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    _Note_: Only Colombian phone numbers with country code \+57 are accepted at this time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 4 — Confirm your phone number">
    A verification code will be sent to your phone via **WhatsApp**.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Get the WhatsApp code">
        Open WhatsApp and find the message with your code.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the code">
        On the **Confirmación del número de teléfono** screen, enter the 4-digit code.

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005392](/images/2026-03-24-005392-1.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    _Did not receive the code? Tap **Reenviar código**._
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 5 — Enter your identity information">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter ID number">
        Enter your **Número de Identificación (cédula)** — no dots, for example: `1234567890`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter name">
        Enter your **Primer Nombre** (first name) and **Apellido** (last name).

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005393](/images/2026-03-24-005393.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Next">
        Click **Siguiente**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Step 6 — Notification preferences">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose notification setting">
        On the **Activar Notificaciones** screen, choose your preference:

        - Click **Aceptar** to enable real-time alerts for orders and updates.
        - Click **Cancelar** to skip for now.

        <Frame>
          ![2026 03 24 005394](/images/2026-03-24-005394.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Finish">
        You will be taken to your dashboard.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Option 1: Sign in with Google

1. Click **Ingresar con Google**.
2. A Google authentication window will open. Select your account and confirm.
3. You will be redirected to your dashboard automatically.

\> If this is your first time, you will go through a short onboarding flow before reaching the dashboard.

---

## Option 2: Sign in with Email

### Step 1 — Enter your email

1. Click **Ingresar con correo electrónico**.
2. On the next screen, enter your email address in the **Correo electrónico** field.
   <Frame>
     ![2026 03 24 005395](/images/2026-03-24-005395-2.png)
   </Frame>
3. Click **Continuar**.

### Step 2 — Verify your email

The platform will send a 6-digit verification code to your email.

1. Check your inbox for the code.
2. On the **Revise su correo** screen, enter the code in the 6-box field.
   <Frame>
     ![2026 03 24 005396](/images/2026-03-24-005396-1.png)
   </Frame>

\> Did not receive the code? Click **Reenviar código** to request a new one.

### Step 3 — Enter your phone number

1. On the **Teléfono móvil** screen, enter your Colombian mobile number.
2. Click **Siguiente**.

<Frame>
  ![2026 03 24 005391](/images/2026-03-24-005391.png)
</Frame>

_Note_: Only Colombian phone numbers with country code \+57 are accepted at this time.

### Step 4 — Confirm your phone number

A verification code will be sent to your phone via **WhatsApp**.

1. Open WhatsApp and find the message with your code.
2. On the **Confirmación del número de teléfono** screen, enter the 4-digit code.
   <Frame>
     ![2026 03 24 005392](/images/2026-03-24-005392-1.png)
   </Frame>

\> Did not receive the code? Tap **Reenviar código**.

### Step 5 — Enter your identity information

1. Enter your **Número de Identificación (cédula)** — no dots, for example: `1234567890`.
2. Enter your **Primer Nombre** (first name) and **Apellido** (last name).
   <Frame>
     ![2026 03 24 005393](/images/2026-03-24-005393.png)
   </Frame>
3. Click **Siguiente**.

### Step 6 — Notification preferences

1. On the **Activar Notificaciones** screen, choose your preference:
   - Click **Aceptar** to enable real-time alerts for orders and updates.
   - Click **Cancelar** to skip for now.
     <Frame>
       ![2026 03 24 005394](/images/2026-03-24-005394.png)
     </Frame>
2. You will be taken to your dashboard.

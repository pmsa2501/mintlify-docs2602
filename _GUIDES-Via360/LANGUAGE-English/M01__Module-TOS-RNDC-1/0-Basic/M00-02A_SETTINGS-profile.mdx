## Feature Overview

Profile Settings lets you review and update your account information in MAM360. It includes a Perfil tab for personal details and a Compañía tab for the companies you belong to.

## Access Settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings (Ajustes)">
    From any page, click **Ajustes** in the left sidebar under the **Usuario** section.

## Settings Tabs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Perfil tab — Personal Information">
    The Perfil tab displays your personal account information.

    | Field | Description |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Nombre** | Your first name |
    | **Apellido** | Your last name |
    | **Correo electrónico** | Your registered email, marked **verificado** |
    | **Teléfono** | Your mobile number, marked **verificado** |
    | **Cédula** | Your national ID number |

    ### Save Changes

    Click **Guardar Cambios** to save any updates.
